Rules for Tuition Reimbursement

Reimbursements are for university courses, seminars, certification prep courses, certifications, technical training and other miscellaneous approved training events.

Reimbursements don't cover materials, like books or equipment.

Reimbursement form must be complete one week prior to start of event.

You may fill out form to get approval from your direct supervisor and department head or you can get this approval directly from them and include it with your submission.

A passing grade or approved presentation for non-graded events must be submitted for approval of reimbursement.

$1000 tuition reimbursement per year is available for every employee.

Below is the available award percentage per event type:

University 80%

Seminars 60%

Certification Preparation 60%

Certification 75%

Technical Training 70%

Other 30%
